What Is My Goal?

I am from Indianapolis, IN, and I started off coding in my Junior year of high school. Computer science has made a big impact on my life and I'm excited to grow and develop my skills furthermore.

My goal in life is to get a career in the computer science field and not only make a career out of it, but to make it a lifestyle and to enjoy what I do. Computer science helps many people get their information out and I want to help people do just that.

Not only do I want to help others but I want to also educate younger girls who are impressionable. Many girls don't understand the importance of the STEM careers, and I want to introduce them to coding the way my teacher introduced it to me.
